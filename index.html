What is the study about?

A team of scientists at Imperial College London would like to find out how people from different demographic backgrounds form friendships. To get real insight, we need real friendships—yours! Take our survey to help our understanding grow.

Is my privacy protected?

Yes. We do not collect any personally identifying information about you or your friends. Your responses are anonymous and are stored behind high-end firewalls. The study has been approved by the Imperial College Research Ethics Committee and has passed high standards.

What's being asked?

We ask you basic questions about your demographic background including your gender, age, the area you live in, your ethnicity, religion, education, occupation, income and the size of your household. We will also ask you to share this survey with your friends on Facebook. All responses are completely voluntary.

Want to get in touch?

Please get in touch if you have any questions or concerns. You can send us an email at friendology@imperial.ac.uk.
